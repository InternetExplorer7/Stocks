Ask for Stock information about various companies

"How is IBM doing?"

"Show me stock information regarding IBM"

"Stocks IBM"

"How's the market treating IBM, Apple and Microsoft?"

NOTE: As long as you limit to 160 chars, you can get data on as many companies from a single message as you want.


[NEW] ask for specific stock information about different companies.

"Volume for Google" - Gets company volume

"Show me volume for Google, IBM, Apple and Yahoo!" - Gets company volume for an array of companies

"Volume and EBITDA for Google and Yahoo" - Gets company volume AND EBITDA for companies

What's the year low for Google - Gets company year low

What's the year high and year low for Apple? - Gets year low and year high for company.

Give me the Volume, EBITDA, year low and year high for google, IBM and Microsoft. -- Gets all 4 pieces of data for each company.

Ask for your own Stock Information

Firing this off sends you stock information for every company that you have ever searched for
